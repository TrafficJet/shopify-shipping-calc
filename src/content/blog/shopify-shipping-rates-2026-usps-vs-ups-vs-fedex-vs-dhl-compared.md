## Shopify Shipping Discounts Depend on Your Plan Tier

Shopify negotiates bulk carrier discounts and passes them directly to you. The discount level depends on your plan: Basic, Grow, Advanced, or Plus. Higher plans unlock steeper discounts — up to 88% off retail carrier rates on certain services (Shopify Shipping, 2026).

These discounts apply automatically when you buy labels inside the Shopify admin at **Settings → Shipping and delivery → Shopify Shipping**. No separate carrier account needed for USPS, UPS, or DHL. You do need Shopify Payments active on your store to access the full discount range. Third-party payment gateways may limit your options.

Merchants switching from post office walk-in rates to Shopify's negotiated rates often save 30–50% on the first batch of labels — even on Basic. Going from Basic to Grow or Advanced saves an additional 5–15% per label depending on carrier and service.

For a closer look at which plan fits your order volume, see our [Shopify plan comparison for 2026](/shopify-plan-comparison-2026).

## USPS Rates on Shopify 2026: The Default Choice for Packages Under 10 lbs

USPS is the cheapest carrier for packages under 10 lbs, especially for residential delivery. Through Shopify, you get discounted rates on Priority Mail, Priority Mail Express, First-Class Package Service (under 13 oz), and Ground Advantage — which replaced several older USPS products in 2023.

### Sample USPS Rates via Shopify (Zone 5, 2026)

| Service | 1 lb | 5 lb | 10 lb |
|---|---|---|---|
| First-Class Package | $4.15 | N/A (>13 oz) | N/A |
| Ground Advantage | $5.30 | $9.85 | $14.20 |
| Priority Mail | $8.15 | $13.40 | $19.75 |
| Priority Mail Express | $27.50 | $34.80 | $43.60 |

*(Shopify Shipping rate cards, 2026. Rates reflect Shopify Grow plan discounts and vary by origin/destination zone.)*

**Cubic Pricing cuts costs 20–40% for small, dense items.** USPS prices Priority Mail Cubic by dimensions rather than weight — as long as the package is under 20 lbs and fits within 0.5 cubic feet. Merchants selling candles, supplements, or small tools regularly save 20–40% compared to standard Priority Mail (USPS, 2026). To use Cubic Pricing through Shopify, select Priority Mail when buying a label. The system applies cubic rates automatically when dimensions qualify.

USPS charges no residential delivery surcharges and no complex fuel surcharge structure like UPS and FedEx do. It also delivers to PO Boxes, military APO/FPO addresses, and every residential address in the country — including rural routes where UPS and FedEx often tack on extra fees. Priority Mail runs 1–3 business days. Ground Advantage runs 2–5 business days.

One real limitation: USPS tracking is less detailed than UPS or FedEx, especially in transit. Customers used to scan-by-scan updates may notice the difference.

## UPS Rates on Shopify 2026: The Breakeven Point Hits Around 10–12 lbs

UPS gets more competitive as package weight goes up — especially for shipments over 10 lbs going to commercial addresses. Shopify offers built-in discounts on UPS Ground, UPS 2-Day Air, and UPS Next Day Air — up to 82% off retail depending on your plan (Shopify Shipping, 2026).

### UPS vs USPS: Heavy Package Comparison (Zone 5, 2026)

| Service | 10 lb | 20 lb |
|---|---|---|
| USPS Ground Advantage | $14.20 | $22.85 |
| UPS Ground (Shopify discounted) | $13.65 | $18.90 |
| UPS Ground (retail) | $24.50 | $35.70 |

*(Shopify Shipping and UPS published rates, 2026. Shopify rates reflect Grow plan discounts.)*

**DIM weight pricing can inflate your UPS costs fast if you're not watching it.** UPS charges whichever is higher: actual weight or a calculated weight based on box size. The formula is (L × W × H) ÷ 139 (in inches).

A throw pillow in a 20" × 20" × 8" box has a DIM weight of roughly 23 lbs — even if the pillow itself weighs 3 lbs. You pay the 23 lb rate. The fix is simple: right-size your packaging. Switching from a generic box to a custom-fit mailer or smaller corrugated box is one of the fastest ways to cut UPS costs.

UPS offers detailed tracking with guaranteed delivery windows, faster transit to commercial addresses, and a solid claims process for lost or damaged shipments. If your customers are mostly businesses, or your average order weighs 10+ lbs, UPS Ground on Shopify will usually beat USPS on cost.

The tradeoff: UPS applies fuel surcharges (updated monthly) and residential delivery surcharges that USPS does not. On a 10 lb residential package, those fees add $2–$4 on top of the base rate. That narrows the advantage.

## FedEx Rates on Shopify 2026: Strongest for Express and Saturday Delivery

FedEx offers several speed-focused services through Shopify: FedEx Ground, FedEx Home Delivery (residential-specific, with Saturday delivery included at no extra charge), FedEx 2Day, and FedEx Overnight. FedEx Ground targets commercial addresses with 1–5 day transit (FedEx, 2026).

### 2-Day Shipping Comparison: FedEx vs UPS (5 lb, Zone 5, 2026)

| Service | Shopify Discounted Rate | Retail Rate |
|---|---|---|
| FedEx 2Day | $18.25 | $38.90 |
| UPS 2-Day Air | $19.10 | $41.50 |

*(Shopify Shipping rate cards and published carrier rates, 2026. Rates reflect Grow plan.)*

FedEx 2Day has a small price edge on mid-weight express shipments. Both carriers add fuel surcharges that change monthly — roughly 7.5% for ground and 9.0% for express as of early 2026 (FedEx and UPS surcharge schedules, 2026).

### Q4 Peak Surcharges Will Hit Your Holiday Margins

FedEx adds per-package surcharges from October through January. These run $1.00–$6.00+ per package during the holiday rush (FedEx, 2025 peak schedule). The pain is worst during Cyber Week and the two weeks before Christmas.

Merchants who offered free shipping in Q4 2025 without building in peak surcharges reported 3–8% margin erosion on those orders, based on Shopify community forum discussions. Plan for this by September — not December.

**FedEx Ground Economy** (formerly SmartPost) hands off final delivery to USPS. It saves money on lightweight residential shipments but adds 2–3 days to transit. Best for low-value, non-urgent orders where price beats speed.

One setup note: connecting FedEx through Shopify may require linking your own FedEx account depending on your plan tier. That adds a step compared to the plug-and-play USPS and UPS integration.

## DHL Rates on Shopify 2026: The Clear Winner for Cross-Border Orders

If you ship outside the US, DHL is worth your attention. Two products are available: **DHL Express** (1–4 business days, premium) and **DHL eCommerce** (5–15 business days, budget). Both are available through Shopify's built-in label purchasing (Shopify Shipping, 2026).

### Sample DHL Express Rates via Shopify (2 lb package, 2026)

| Destination | DHL Express (Shopify) | USPS Priority Mail International |
|---|---|---|
| US → Canada | $24.50 | $32.80 |
| US → UK | $33.75 | $46.25 |
| US → Australia | $38.90 | $52.10 |

*(Shopify Shipping rate cards, 2026. Rates reflect Grow plan discounts and may vary by zip code.)*

DHL Express beats USPS Priority Mail International on both cost and speed for most destinations. DHL also offers upfront duties and taxes calculation through its **Delivered Duty Paid (DDP)** service — meaning the seller covers all customs fees before delivery, so the buyer pays nothing extra at the door. This removes surprise charges at customs, which is one of the main reasons international orders get refused.

A 2023 Baymard Institute study on checkout usability found that unexpected costs — including shipping and duties — are the number one reason for cart abandonment, cited by 48% of respondents. DDP removes that friction point entirely for international buyers.

**DHL eCommerce is the budget option** when your international customers will wait. Rates run 40–60% below DHL Express, but delivery stretches to 1–3 weeks. Tracking detail is also thinner during the international transit leg. For a full walkthrough, see our [Shopify international shipping guide](/shopify-international-shipping-guide).

## How to Choose the Right Carrier: A Multi-Carrier Framework

Base your decision on three things: average package weight, domestic vs. international order mix, and how fast your customers expect delivery.

Most merchants do better with multiple carriers than with one. Here's how to split your volume:

- **Under 13 oz:** USPS First-Class Package — cheapest domestic option by a wide margin
- **13 oz – 10 lbs:** USPS Ground Advantage or Priority Mail
- **Over 10 lbs domestic:** UPS Ground (or FedEx Ground for commercial addresses)
- **2-day express:** FedEx 2Day — slight price edge over UPS 2-Day Air
- **International, speed matters:** DHL Express
- **International, cost matters:** DHL eCommerce

To show real-time carrier rates at checkout, go to **Settings → Shipping and delivery → Carrier-calculated rates** in your Shopify admin. This is included with Advanced and Plus plans. Basic and Grow plan users can add it for $1/month, or use a third-party app like ShipStation, Shippo, or EasyPost to display live rates.

Our [carrier-calculated shipping guide](/shopify-carrier-calculated-shipping) walks through setup step by step. For app recommendations, see [best Shopify shipping apps 2026](/best-shopify-shipping-apps-2026).

> **Real merchant example:** A Shopify Grow plan store selling handmade soaps — average package weight 12 oz — was paying $11.40 per label using FedEx Home Delivery. After switching to USPS Ground Advantage through Shopify Shipping, the average label cost dropped to $5.30. On 200 orders a month, that's roughly $1,220 in savings. Transit time went up by about one day on average. Customer satisfaction scores, measured by post-purchase survey, stayed flat. The lesson: speed only matters if your customers expect it.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which Shopify plan gives the best shipping discounts in 2026?
Shopify Advanced and Plus offer the highest discounts — up to 88% off retail carrier rates. Even Basic unlocks real savings over walk-in post office or UPS Store rates. The per-label difference between Basic and Advanced is typically $0.50–$2.00. The upgrade only pays for itself if your monthly order volume is high enough to cover the plan cost difference.

### Does Shopify offer discounted rates for all four carriers?
Shopify has built-in discounts for USPS, UPS, and DHL. FedEx discounts are available but may require linking your own FedEx account through **Settings → Shipping and delivery → Carrier accounts**, depending on your plan.

### Is USPS or UPS cheaper for a 5 lb domestic package on Shopify?
For a 5 lb package under 12 inches on each side, USPS Ground Advantage is typically cheaper — $9.85 vs. $11.40 for UPS Ground at Zone 5 on the Grow plan, as of 2026. UPS Ground usually wins for packages over 10 lbs or when shipping to commercial addresses where the residential surcharge doesn't apply.

### What is dimensional weight and how does it affect my Shopify shipping costs?
Dimensional weight is a calculated weight based on box size, using the formula (L × W × H) ÷ 139 (in inches). UPS and FedEx charge whichever is higher — actual weight or DIM weight — on all services. USPS applies DIM weight only to Priority Mail Express boxes over one cubic foot. Right-sizing your boxes is the most direct way to avoid DIM weight overcharges.

### Can I use DHL for domestic US shipments through Shopify?
DHL eCommerce handles some domestic last-mile delivery in the US, but DHL Express is built for international shipments. For domestic packages, USPS, UPS, and FedEx are better on both cost and transit time.

### How do I enable carrier-calculated shipping rates at Shopify checkout?
Go to **Settings → Shipping and delivery → Carrier-calculated rates** in your Shopify admin. Included with Advanced and Plus plans. Basic and Grow plan users can add it for $1/month or use third-party apps like Shippo or ShipStation to show live rates at checkout.

### Do Shopify shipping rates change during the year?
Yes. USPS, UPS, and FedEx raise rates annually in January — 4.9–5.9% in 2026. FedEx and UPS also add peak surcharges from October through January, running $1–$6+ per package. Fuel surcharges change monthly. Always check current rates in your Shopify Shipping dashboard before setting your store's shipping prices.
